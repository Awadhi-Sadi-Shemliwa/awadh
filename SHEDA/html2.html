Operations & Products

Sheda General Supplies specializes in the production of forest products, including timber and a wide variety of wooden poles. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our wooden poles are crafted to meet the highest standards, ensuring durability and reliability. We specifically produce electrical poles, telephone poles, and fencing poles, catering to the diverse needs of our clients.

Sheda General Supplies has established itself as a leading producer in the industry. Our extensive experience and dedication to excellence have earned us the trust of customers across the region. We are proud to deliver high-quality products, timely service, and competitive pricing, making us a preferred choice for wooden poles in the market. .
